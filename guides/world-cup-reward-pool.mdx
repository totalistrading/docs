Trade the FIFA World Cup season for a share of the **\$10,000** Totalis reward pool. You're eligible if you bet on World Cup markets.

## How it works

On **July 20th**, the full **\$10,000** pool is shared across everyone who qualified. You qualify by placing World Cup parlays (parlays where at least **50% of the legs** consist of World Cup events).

## How rewards are calculated

Rewards are distributed on a weighted-average basis by eligible volume.
