Trade the FIFA World Cup season for a share of the **\$10,000** Totalis reward pool. You're eligible if you bet on World Cup markets.

## How it works

On **July 20th**, you're ranked by the total bet amount you placed on World Cup parlays (parlays where at least **50% of the legs** consist of World Cup events).

## Prize pool distribution

The \$10,000 prize pool is distributed as follows:

| Rank | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1st place | \$3,000 |
| 2nd place | \$2,000 |
| 3rd place | \$1,000 |
| 4th–10th place | \$200 each |
| Everyone else | Share the remaining \$2,600, weighted by volume |

Everyone outside the top 10 splits the remaining **\$2,600** on a volume-weighted basis — your share is your eligible volume as a percentage of the total eligible volume across all qualifying participants outside the top 10.
