Trade the FIFA World Cup season for a share of the **\$10,000** Totalis reward pool. You're eligible if you bet on World Cup markets.

## How it works

On **July 20th**, the full **\$10,000** pool is shared across everyone who qualified. You qualify by placing World Cup parlays (parlays where at least **50% of the legs** consist of World Cup events).

## How rewards are calculated

Your reward is based on your eligible volume relative to everyone else's — there's no fixed payout per \$1 traded. Your share of the pool is your weighted eligible volume as a percentage of the total weighted eligible volume across all qualifying participants.

In short, if others trade more, your share goes down; if you trade more relative to the field, your share goes up. The final payout depends on how volume is distributed across all participants.
