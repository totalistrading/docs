The FIFA World Cup is here! We want to see who can make the best parlays across the World Cup season. **\$10,000** will be distributed to everyone that bet on World Cup markets.

## How it works

On **July 20th**, we will rank users by the total bet amount placed on World Cup parlays (parlays where at least **50% of the legs** consist of World Cup events).

## Prize pool distribution

The \$10,000 prize pool will be distributed as follows:

| Rank | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1st place | \$3,000 |
| 2nd place | \$2,000 |
| 3rd place | \$1,000 |
| 4th–10th place | \$200 each |
| Everyone else | Split the remaining \$2,600 |

Everyone else that placed at least one World Cup parlay will split the remaining **\$2,600** of the reward pool.
