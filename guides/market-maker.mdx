Market makers on Totalis monitor incoming parlay RFQs, submit competitive quotes, and earn the spread when users accept. There is no separate market maker role — any authenticated user can quote on open RFQs.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect via WebSocket">
    Subscribe to the `rfqs:open` channel to receive new parlay requests instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Quotes">
    Analyze the legs and submit your payout odds before the RFQ expires.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Trades">
    When a user accepts your quote, confirm before the deadline to lock in the trade via onchain Solana vaults.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Core Quoting Loop

For most market makers, the public integration surface should stay small:

| Step | Endpoint |
| --- | --- |
| Pull market context and current prices | [`GET /markets`](/api-reference/markets/list) |
| Check invalid leg combinations | [`GET /markets/exclusion-groups`](/api-reference/markets/exclusion-groups) |
| Receive new RFQs | [`rfqs:open` WebSocket channel](/guides/websocket) or [`GET /mm/rfqs`](/api-reference/mm/list-rfqs) |
| Submit a quote | [`POST /mm/quotes`](/api-reference/mm/submit-quote) |
| Track your quotes | [`GET /mm/quotes`](/api-reference/mm/list-quotes) |
| Confirm an accepted quote | [`POST /mm/quotes/{quoteId}/confirm`](/api-reference/mm/confirm-quote) |
| Pull stale risk | [`POST /mm/quotes/{quoteId}/withdraw`](/api-reference/mm/withdraw-quote) |

Use [`GET /user/wallet`](/api-reference/user/get-wallet) to check available USDC and locked funds before quoting at size.
