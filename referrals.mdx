---
title: 'Referrals'
description: 'Earn a share of the trading fees from the users you refer'
---

Refer other traders and earn a cut of the fees they generate. Anyone who signs up through your referral link is tied to your account, and you earn 20% of the taker fees on their trades for as long as they keep trading.

## How to refer someone

Open Settings and go to the Referrals tab. Copy your referral link and share it. When someone signs up through that link they appear in your referred users list, and they start earning you rewards the moment they trade.

## What you earn

You earn 20% of the taker fee on every trade your referred users make. The taker fee is what gets charged when a trade fills, so your reward is a straight cut of fees that were actually collected, paid in USDC. It builds up as a claimable balance in your account.

A few things worth knowing:

- You earn only from the people you refer directly. There are no extra tiers.
- Rewards accrue on both settled parlays and early cashouts, since both charge a taker fee.
- Your rate is fixed per reward at the time it accrues, so a later rate change never touches what you already earned.

## Claiming your rewards

The Referrals tab shows three numbers: what you have earned in total, what is available to claim right now, and what you have already been paid.

Once your claimable balance reaches the 25 USDC minimum, the Claim button turns on. Press it and the USDC is sent to your wallet. The payout usually confirms within a few seconds, and the tab updates once it lands.

If your balance is under 25 USDC, nothing is lost. It stays in your account and keeps growing as your referred users trade, and you can claim as soon as it clears the minimum.

## Good to know

- Payouts go to the wallet linked to your account, so make sure you have one set up before you claim.
- You can have one claim in flight at a time. If a payout is still processing, let it finish before starting another.
- If a claim sits as pending for a little while, it is safe. Your balance is never lost, and the payout completes once it settles.
