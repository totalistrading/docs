---
title: 'List Markets (Flat)'
description: 'A flat, paginated feed of every individual market — ungrouped and never truncated. Built for market makers and partners syncing the full tradeable universe.'
openapi: 'GET /v1/markets/list'
---

<Note>
  **Building parlays?** Every market carries an `exclusion_keys` array. Two
  markets can share a parlay only if their arrays don't intersect — a plain
  set-intersection check, with no correlation rules to maintain client-side.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Need every market for a single game? `GET /markets/events/{event_ticker}`
  returns one event's complete market set in a single request.
</Tip>
